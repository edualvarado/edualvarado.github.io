Background

I'm Eduardo and I hold a master’s degree in Embedded Systems Engineering, with a major in Artificial Intelligence and Autonomous Intelligent Systems from the Albert-Ludwigs-Universität Freiburg. I also have a bachelor's degree in Electronics and Automation Engineering from Universidad Carlos III in Madrid. Now I find myself pursuing my greatest aspiration: to do research in the field of AI for Computer Graphics.

I have dedicated my academic career to the field of AI, with special interest in Perception and Behavior Planning. I wrote my master's thesis at Robert Bosch GmbH on DL sensor-fusion architectures for Object Tracking, under the supervision from Prof. Dr. Joschka Boedecker and Prof. Dr. Abnihav Valada. I worked as intern at ZF Friedrichshafen too, on the development of ROS tools for self-driving vehicles. Additionally, I have been working as research assistant for Autonomous Systems, on SLAM algorithms and Autonomous Driving simulations with Unreal Engine.

On the other side, I dedicate my day-to-day to my other great passion, Computer Graphics and Game-dev. Gradually, I learn more about 3D frameworks such as OpenGL, game-engines like Unreal Engine and Unity, and 3D design, trying to bring AI with 3D scenes and animations together.
